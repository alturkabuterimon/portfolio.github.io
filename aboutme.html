About Me

My name is Ashleigh Kennedy and I'm a recent graduate of the Creative Computing (Gaming) course at Bath Spa University. I've had an interest in computers and games from a young age and gained an interest in programming when I took it as a class in college. I like games that are artistic in nature and like aiming the games I make to be memorable experiences. I'm also an avid artist and have been drawing seriously from the age of 11. My art can be found primarily on my Instagram. I enjoy RPGs, space shooter and exploration games. Particular faves are the Persona series, the Touhou series and the game Journey.
